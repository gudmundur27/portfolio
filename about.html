About the Photographer

Hi! I'm Guðmundur Hermann Þórlaugarson from Grindavík, Iceland. I'm a passionate photographer with a love for capturing the thrill and emotion of sports. Whether it's the intensity of a football match, the speed of a cycling race, or the energy of a local event, I strive to freeze those moments that tell a story.

While sports photography is my main focus, I love exploring all genres—from landscapes to portraits and everything in between. Every shoot is a new adventure and a chance to see the world from a fresh perspective.

“Great photography is about depth of feeling, not depth of field.”

Thanks for visiting my portfolio. If you want to collaborate, have an event to capture, or just want to chat about photography, feel free to get in touch!
